# 🔐 Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

- Never share your UPI PIN, OTP, or banking details with anyone
- Do not click on unknown or suspicious links
- Always verify the identity of the caller before taking any action
- Do not approve payment requests unless you are sending money
- Avoid scanning QR codes from unknown sources
- Enable app notifications and transaction alerts
- Use strong passwords and enable two-factor authentication
- Regularly check your bank statements for unusual activity

## 🚨 Report Cybercrime
- National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in

# 📋 Social Media Do’s and Don’ts

## ✅ Do’s
- Maintain a professional and clean profile
- Share useful, informative, and positive content
- Respect others’ opinions and communicate politely
- Keep your privacy settings updated and secure
- Verify information before sharing it online

---

## ❌ Don’ts
- Don’t share personal or sensitive information (phone number, passwords, etc.)
- Don’t post offensive, harmful, or inappropriate content
- Don’t spread fake news or unverified information
- Don’t engage in unnecessary arguments or online conflicts
- Don’t use unprofessional language or slang in public posts

---

## 🎯 Note
Responsible use of social media helps in building a positive digital identity and ensures safe online interaction.
